The Studio

Design with purpose, executed with precision.

Alimo Architects is a Los Angeles practice working across luxury residences, multifamily housing and commercial developments. We treat every project — from a single home to a mixed-use building — with the same focus on quality, sustainability and how a space is actually lived in.

From first consultation to final permit, we keep the process clear and collaborative, translating each client's vision into architecture that is environmentally conscious and built to last.
